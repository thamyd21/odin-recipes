Mantu

A traditional savoury Afghan dish

Seasoned beef and onions, tucked away in a tender dumpling wrapping, and topped with an herby yogurt sauce make this a classic Afghan dish.

"A unique Afghan take on dumplings"- Chef's Opinion

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon dried mint
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 Lbs ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 3 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • Pack of wonton wrappers (40-50 wontons)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix yogurt, dried mint, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, and salt to taste
  2. Heat olive oil in large skillet. Add beef, 1 teaspoon salt, ground coriander, 2 teaspoons garlic, and pepper to taste. Cook until beef is fully cooked through
  3. Remove skillet from heat and stir in onion powder
  4. Place spoonful of beef stuffing in a wonton. Lightly water the edges to make them sticky. Shape the edges together into a dumpling. Repeat until stuffing is gone
  5. Brush bottom of steamer pot lightly with oil. Place dumplings in pot. Cover and steam until dumplings are tender and slightly translucent, about 8 minutes. Repeat with remaining dumplings
  6. Remove steamed dumplings from pot and serve on plate or platter. Top with the herby yogurt sauce
