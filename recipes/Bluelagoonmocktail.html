Blue Lagoon Mocktail

A refreshing sweet summery drink

A summer classic with a refreshing cirtusy flavour. The blue lagoon mocktail is a cross between lemonade and a blue hue.

"The perfect summer drink for those really hot days"- Chef's Opinion

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Blue curacao syrup
  • Freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Sprite
  • Ice cubes
  • Cherry (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a hurricane glas with ice cubes
  2. Add 1/2 oz blue curacao syrup and 1 oz of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  3. Top with 4 oz sprite and stir gently
  4. Add a cherry to the lip of the hurricane glass for the complete look
